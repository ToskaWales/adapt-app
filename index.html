How To Use ADDAPT
Check in, train, log, improve
The app works best when you run the loop: update your status, follow the plan, log your lifts, then let ADDAPT adjust the next block around what actually happened.
1
Check-In drives the plan
Energy, sleep, diet, lifts and bodyweight feed the adaptive engine so calories and training mode stay aligned with your real week.
2
Training and analytics stay connected
Current Plan shows the split, Log Session stores performance, and Strength Progress turns that into PR tracking, charts and useful weight history.
3
Recovery gets its own system
Sauna Calculator gives a guide for cardio, stress, recovery or longevity with heat protocol, timer, hydration and weekly placement.
